Damp Pulsatance Prototype

I have some fully-functional Damp Pulsatance prototypes complete with all the labels and knobs that the final version has. If anyone is interested I can sell these at close to cost prices.

Here are the deficiencies of the prototype models:

  • The Centre Off control is effected by the depth control - the Depth control has to be at 12 o'clock to get the full range of Centre Off control.

  • The input impedance is ~ 733 kΩ rather than the target 1 MΩ. When the effect is bypassed the input impedance drops to ~ 88 kΩ due to a mistake in the bypass circuit.

In order to fix some mistakes on the PCB itself there are a few bodge wires, but these don't have any impact on the performance.
